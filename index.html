the chikhli uraban vidyaniketan
started from 2014

Leaders from all walks of life agree that a soiled education is the foundation of a bought future. However, 400-500 students from Chikhli have to travel 25 km everyday to arm them with this foundation. The nearest CBSE English medium school is in Buldhana and for these young minds, it entailed a travel which was difficult, discouraging. In order to grant their children the opportunities and facilities that would shape bright futures. The residents of Chikhli decided to join hands. The employees, directors and local advisory members of the Chikhli urban Co-operative Bank came together and pooled in their resources on a model similar to Credit Agree Goal, a subsidiary of yes Bank in Paris. They established a multi-Faceted-Organization aiming to undertake Social Projects, Including Education. They surveyed people from various sections of society to establish the most important factors that would enhance the education of the next generation. The CBSC school in Chikhli aims to fill this gap and empower the youth of Chikhli. The School provides all comforts, tenderness and care that the child needs.
